What Torque Hub Offers Dealers

Torque Hub is a commercial marketplace built specifically for trucks, trailers, and equipment. Every buyer on the platform is looking for commercial inventory — not a family sedan.

Inventory Exposure

Your listings appear on a dedicated commercial marketplace with SEO-optimized pages, category filters, and a buyer base actively searching for commercial trucks, trailers, and equipment.

Built-In Financing Funnel

Every listing connects directly to a commercial financing application. Buyers can apply for financing from your listing page — increasing conversion and reducing buyer hesitation on high-ticket units.

Direct Buyer Contact

Phone number and contact links on every listing. Buyers call you directly — no lead resale, no middleman taking a cut of your deal.

Social Content Amplification

Torque Hub is powered by Torque DMA, a full-service digital marketing operation serving commercial dealers across the Southeast. Dealer partners benefit from active social distribution of their inventory.

Who This Is For

Commercial Truck Dealers

Dealers selling dump trucks, flatbeds, semi trucks, box trucks, rollbacks, and other commercial work vehicles.

Trailer Dealers

Full-service trailer dealerships selling livestock trailers, dump trailers, equipment trailers, enclosed trailers, and specialty haulers.

Equipment Dealers

Dealers and auctioneers selling skid steers, excavators, tractors, forklifts, and heavy construction or agricultural equipment.

Independent Sellers

Owner-operators and small operations with commercial inventory to sell who want exposure beyond local classifieds and Facebook groups.
