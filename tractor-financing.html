Frequently Asked Questions

Can I finance a used tractor?

Yes. Financing is available for quality used tractors. Lenders evaluate hours, condition, and age alongside your credit and income.

What types of tractors can be financed?

Utility tractors, row crop tractors, compact tractors, and specialty agricultural tractors. Most major brands including John Deere, Case IH, New Holland, Kubota, and Massey Ferguson.

Do I need a farm to finance a tractor?

Not necessarily. Contractors, landscapers, and property owners also finance tractors for various uses. Agricultural programs may require farm verification.

Can I get tractor financing with bad credit?

Yes. Agricultural equipment financing often has more flexible credit requirements because the tractor serves as strong collateral with broad resale demand.

How fast is approval?

Most tractor financing approvals happen within minutes to a few hours for smaller loans. Larger agricultural purchases may take one business day.
