Frequently Asked Questions

Can I finance a used dump truck?

Yes. Financing is available for quality used dump trucks. Most lenders look at the truck condition, age, and mileage alongside your credit and income when making a decision.

What credit score do I need to finance a dump truck?

We work with all credit situations including buyers with scores below 600. A solid down payment or strong income history can help offset a lower credit score with many commercial lenders.

Can I finance a dump truck for a new business?

Yes. Some lenders specialize in startup operators and new businesses entering the hauling or contracting industry. Come with your situation and we will find the right fit.

How much do I need to put down on a dump truck?

Down payment requirements vary by lender and credit profile. Some programs are available with little to no money down. A larger down payment generally improves your rate and approval odds.

How fast is approval?

Most approvals happen within minutes to a few hours. We move fast so the truck doesn't go to another buyer while you are waiting.
