Credit and Down Payment

Commercial truck financing is different from consumer auto loans. Lenders weigh multiple factors — not just your credit score.

All Credit Situations Considered

We work with buyers across the full credit spectrum, including scores below 600. A strong down payment or income history can offset a lower score in many cases.

Down Payment

Programs vary. Some lenders offer low or no money down options. A larger down payment typically improves your rate and terms. Come with what you have — we will work with it.

Time in Business

Startups and new operators are welcome. Some programs are designed specifically for buyers who are just getting started in commercial hauling or contracting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I finance a used commercial truck?

Yes. Most of the commercial trucks on Torque Hub are quality used units. Financing is available for both new and used trucks depending on the lender and vehicle condition.

What types of trucks can be financed?

Dump trucks, flatbeds, box trucks, rollbacks, tractor-trailers, service trucks, and other commercial vehicles. If it works for a living, we can typically help finance it.

Do I need a CDL to finance a commercial truck?

Not always. Requirements vary by lender and truck type. Many smaller commercial trucks do not require a CDL. Our team can help match you with the right lender based on your situation.

Can I get approved with bad credit?

Yes. We work with all credit situations. Credit score is one factor, but down payment, income, and the truck itself also matter. Don't let your score stop you from applying.

How fast can I get approved?

Most approvals happen within minutes to a few hours. We move fast so you don't lose the truck to another buyer.
