Frequently Asked Questions

Can I finance a used dump trailer?

Yes. Quality used dump trailers are financeable. Lenders look at hydraulic condition, frame integrity, and overall trailer condition.

What size dump trailers can be financed?

Small 5x8 dump trailers to large gooseneck dump trailers. Size and price point determine available programs.

Do I need a business to finance a dump trailer?

Not always. Both individuals and businesses can finance dump trailers depending on the lender and intended use.

How fast is approval?

Dump trailer financing is among the fastest commercial equipment financing. Many approvals happen within the hour.

Can I finance a dump trailer with bad credit?

Yes. Dump trailers are lower price points and strong collateral, making them more accessible for challenged credit buyers.
