Frequently Asked Questions

Can I finance a used semi truck?

Yes. Most semi trucks on Torque Hub are quality used units. Lenders evaluate age, mileage, and condition alongside your credit profile.

Can a new owner-operator get financed?

Yes. Some lenders specialize in first-time owner-operators. A solid down payment and CDL can strengthen your application significantly.

Do I need a CDL to finance a semi truck?

Most lenders require a valid CDL for Class 8 financing. Some programs allow financing pending CDL completion.

What credit score is needed?

We work with all credit situations. Revenue history, CDL status, and down payment all factor into semi truck financing decisions.

How fast is approval?

Most approvals happen within a few hours. Class 8 loans may require additional documentation depending on loan size.
