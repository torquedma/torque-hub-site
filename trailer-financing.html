Frequently Asked Questions

Can I finance a used trailer?

Yes. Financing is available for both new and used trailers depending on the lender and trailer condition. Most trailers on Torque Hub are quality used units.

What types of trailers can be financed?

Dump trailers, enclosed trailers, equipment trailers, flatbeds, utility trailers, livestock trailers, gooseneck trailers, car haulers, and race trailers. If it hauls, we can typically help you finance it.

Do I need a business to finance a trailer?

Not always. Some lenders work with individuals purchasing trailers for personal or farm use. Commercial use trailers may have different program options available.

What credit score is needed to finance a trailer?

We work with all credit situations. Trailers are often easier to finance than larger commercial vehicles because of their lower price points and broad collateral value.

How long does approval take?

Most approvals happen within minutes to a few hours. Trailers are straightforward to finance and lenders typically move quickly on decisions.
