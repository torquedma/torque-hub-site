Frequently Asked Questions

What size is a mini excavator?

Mini excavators typically range from 1 to 6 tons. They are designed for tight access and light to medium duty excavation.

Can I finance a used mini excavator?

Yes. Used mini excavators are commonly financed. Lenders look at hours, condition, and brand.

Can a new business finance a mini excavator?

Yes. Lower price points make mini excavators more accessible for new businesses and first-time equipment buyers.

What brands of mini excavator can be financed?

Most major brands including Kubota, John Deere, Bobcat, Cat, Takeuchi, Yanmar, and others.

How fast is approval?

Mini excavator financing often happens within hours due to lower loan amounts and strong collateral value.
