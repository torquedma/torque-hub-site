Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Torque Hub ("we," "us," or "our") is a commercial vehicle and equipment marketplace operated as a service of Torque DMA, a sole proprietorship based in North Carolina. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit hub.torquedma.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the site.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide. When you submit a financing inquiry or contact a dealer, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and any optional details you choose to share (such as the unit you're interested in or notes about your purchase plans).
  • Automatic information. When you browse the site, our servers and analytics tools automatically receive technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, referring page, pages viewed, time spent, and approximate location based on IP.
  • Cookies and tracking technologies. We use cookies and similar technologies (including pixels and tags from third-party services described below) to remember preferences, measure site performance, and improve the user experience.

How We Use Information

  • To connect you with the dealer or lender you've requested information from.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the site, including diagnosing problems and analyzing usage trends.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • To comply with applicable laws and respond to lawful requests.

Financing Inquiries

If you submit a financing inquiry through Torque Hub, we collect only your contact information (name, phone, email) and basic details about your interest. We do not collect Social Security numbers, full credit application data, or other sensitive financial information through this site. When you are routed to a lending partner to complete a full credit application, that application is submitted directly to the lender on their own website. The lender's own privacy policy governs the information they collect, and we encourage you to review it.

How We Share Information

We share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With dealers and lenders. When you submit an inquiry about a specific unit or request financing, we share your contact information with the relevant dealer or lender so they can follow up with you.
  • With service providers. We use third-party services to operate the site, including hosting, analytics, and email delivery. These providers process information on our behalf under contractual obligations.
  • Legal compliance. We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Torque Hub, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers. If Torque Hub is acquired, merged, or sells assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their independent marketing purposes.

Third-Party Analytics and Advertising

We use the following third-party tools, which collect information directly from your browser through cookies and tracking pixels:

  • Google Analytics — to understand site usage and improve performance. Google's privacy practices are described at policies.google.com/privacy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel — to measure the effectiveness of our advertising on Facebook and Instagram. Meta's practices are described at facebook.com/privacy/policy. You can manage ad preferences in your Facebook settings.

These services may combine the information they collect with information from other sites you visit. We do not control what these third parties do with the information they receive directly.

Your Choices

  • Cookies. Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.
  • Marketing communications. If you receive marketing emails from us, you can unsubscribe using the link in any message.
  • Access, correction, and deletion. You can request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you by emailing us at the address below. We will respond within a reasonable time and in accordance with applicable law.
  • Do Not Track. Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" signal. We currently do not respond to these signals because there is no industry standard for doing so.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Inquiry data is generally retained for up to 24 months unless a longer period is required.

Children's Privacy

Torque Hub is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
