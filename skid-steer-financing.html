Skid steers and track loaders are among the most commonly financed pieces of construction equipment because they generate revenue immediately across a wide range of applications.

Wheeled Skid Steers

Standard wheeled skid steers for job sites where maneuverability and speed matter. Bobcat, Case, Cat, John Deere, New Holland, and other major brands.

Compact Track Loaders

Rubber track loaders for soft ground, slopes, and sensitive surfaces. Better traction and flotation than wheeled machines in challenging conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I finance a used skid steer?

Yes. Financing is available for quality used skid steers. Lenders evaluate hours, condition, and age alongside your credit when underwriting.

What brands can be financed?

Most major brands including Bobcat, Case, Caterpillar, John Deere, New Holland, Kubota, and others. Brand matters less than condition and hours.

Can a startup business finance a skid steer?

Yes. Equipment financing for new businesses is available through several lenders. The machine serves as collateral which improves accessibility.

How many hours is too many for skid steer financing?

It depends on the lender and the machine condition. Well-maintained machines with higher hours can still be financed. The full picture matters more than hours alone.

How fast is approval?

Most skid steer financing approvals happen within minutes to a few hours. Compact equipment is well-understood by lenders and moves quickly.
