Lender Partner FAQ

If I help bring inventory onto Torque Hub, can financing opportunities stay connected to me?

Torque Hub is being built with source attribution in mind. When a lending partner helps bring inventory into the marketplace, related buyer activity can be tracked so financing opportunities are easier to connect back to the right partner relationship.

Do you work with lenders outside the Southeast?

Yes. Torque Hub serves buyers nationally and works with lenders who operate across multiple states.

What credit profiles do your buyers have?

We serve buyers across all credit situations — prime, near-prime, subprime, startups, and established businesses.

How are applications connected to lender partners?

Torque Hub is being built around source attribution. When a lender helps bring inventory or dealer relationships into the marketplace, related buyer activity can be tracked back to that source relationship.
