High-Intent Commercial Buyers

The Buyer Profile

Owner-Operators

Independent operators buying their first or next commercial truck. Strong earning potential, varying credit profiles.

Small Contractors

Construction, landscaping, and trade contractors buying dump trucks, equipment trailers, and work vehicles.

Farmers and Ag Operators

Agricultural buyers shopping tractors, livestock trailers, and farm equipment through our dealer network.

Small Fleet Operators

Businesses with 1-10 trucks adding units to their commercial fleet. Established revenue, growth-stage financing needs.

Asset Classes

Commercial Trucks

Dump trucks, flatbeds, semi trucks, box trucks, rollbacks, service trucks, and other Class 3-8 commercial vehicles.

Trailers

Equipment trailers, livestock trailers, dump trailers, enclosed trailers, gooseneck trailers, and flatbed trailers.
