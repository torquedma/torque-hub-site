What We Finance

Commercial Truck Financing

Whether you need a dump truck, box truck, flatbed, rollback, or tractor-trailer, Torque Hub connects you with lenders who specialize in commercial vehicle financing. New businesses, owner-operators, and established fleets are all welcome. Most approvals happen within minutes — not days.

Equipment Financing

Skid steers, wheel loaders, excavators, forklifts, tractors, and other work equipment. Equipment financing lets you put machines to work before they're fully paid off — turning your financing into a revenue tool, not just a debt. All credit situations considered.

Trailer Financing

Livestock trailers, enclosed trailers, dump trailers, flatbed trailers, hoppers, dry vans, and race trailers. Trailer financing is available for both new and used units. If it hauls, we can help you finance it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does applying affect my credit score?

No. Submitting your application through Torque Hub is intended for initial matching. If a lender needs a hard credit pull, you should be notified before that step.

What credit score do I need?

We work with all credit situations — including buyers with scores below 600. Credit score is one factor, but income, down payment, and the unit itself also matter. Don't let your credit score stop you from applying.

Can I finance a used work truck?

Yes. Most of the inventory on Torque Hub is quality used commercial vehicles. Financing is available for both new and used trucks, trailers, and equipment depending on the lender and unit condition.

How long does approval take?

Most approvals happen within minutes to a few hours. In some cases it may take one business day depending on the lender and documentation needed. We move fast so you don't lose the unit.

Do I need a down payment?

It depends on the lender and your credit profile. Some programs are available with little to no money down. A down payment can improve your rate and terms, but it is not always required to get approved.

Can I finance equipment for my business?

Yes. We finance commercial equipment including skid steers, excavators, loaders, forklifts, and agricultural equipment. Business financing is available for established businesses as well as startups.
